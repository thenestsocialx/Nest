a note from who built this

We built this because we needed it too.

nest started from a very real, very personal understanding of what it's like to fall apart quietly — and not know where to turn.

A few years ago, I went through a period I can only describe as quietly falling apart. I wasn't in crisis — not the kind anyone could see. I was showing up, functioning, saying the right things. But something was deeply off, and I didn't know how to ask for help without feeling like too much.

I looked for something that felt human — not clinical, not cold, not designed for the version of me that already had it figured out. I couldn't find it. So we built it.

nest is for everyone who's fine on the outside and struggling on the inside. For the 3am thoughts, the lonely Sundays, the "I should be over this by now" spiral. We built this for you — and honestly, for the person I was back then.

With a lot of care,
Founder, nest
